Why we worked with Flower and White

Flower and White is a yet upcoming brand racing their way to the top. We decided to share our service with the company to help them reach where they want to be. But what inspired us most was the fact that they do not use harmful toxins to create their products, in fact, they use nothing but natural resources to help and make the world a better place, they deserve all the help they can get !

Who is Flower and White

Two main things you should know about us. Yes, we really did start in a shed making giant Swiss meringues and yes, like you, we absolutely love our chocolate. But to be brutally honest when we started our business, we didn’t have the faintest idea that putting these 2 things together might magic up a 95 calorie chocolate bar of such epic proportions. Glad we did though and rest assured we’ll keep coming up with more.

Flower and White has always been clear on a few other things. They want their business to be fun, to never take themselves too seriously; to always do business the right way; and to make irresistible treats that make people happy. They wouldn’t have it any other way!

Creating a sustainable database

Our client reached out to us to provide them with a sustainable database and we just couldn't say no to such a remarkable request. We used MySQL to create the database, which they are currently using. Since they have a well functioning webpage, they store all their products in that specific database. They loved it and therefore, made our day !
